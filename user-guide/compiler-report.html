Easy Integration of the GWT Compile Report in your Maven Site

You must activate the compiler mojo option compileReport 

<project>
  [...]
  <build>
    <plugins>
      [...]
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.codehaus.mojo</groupId>
        <artifactId>gwt-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>2.8.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
        <configuration>
          <compileReport>true</compileReport>
        </configuration>
      </plugin>
      [...]
    </plugins>
  </build>
  [...]
  <reporting>
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.codehaus.mojo</groupId>
        <artifactId>gwt-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>2.8.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
        <reportSets>
          <reportSet>
            <reports>
              <report>compile-report</report>
            </reports>
          </reportSet>
        </reportSets>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
  </reporting>  
</project>
